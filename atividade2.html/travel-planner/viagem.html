My Dream Destination: Bali, Indonesia

Bali has always been my dream destination due to its stunning beaches, rich culture, and vibrant nightlife. I am excited to explore its beautiful landscapes and immerse myself in its traditions.

Places to Visit

  1. Uluwatu Temple
  2. Rice Terraces of Tegallalang
  3. Mount Batur
  4. Seminyak Beach
  5. Monkey Forest in Ubud

Items to Pack

  • Sunscreen
  • Swimwear
  • Lightweight clothing
  • Camera
  • Travel guide
